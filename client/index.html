Tutorial


Guess the word in 12 tries.

Each guess must be a valid four-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

Unlike the original wordle, this version of worlde validates your guess by telling you if the actual answer is alphabetically before or after your guess.

Example

C
L
A
W
C
A
T
S
The answer is CATS, and the guess is CLAW.

Since C is the correct alphabet, C is shaded green.

Since L is alphabetically after A, L is shaded red.

Since A is alphabetically before T, A is shaded yellow.

Since W is alphabetically after S, W is shaded red.
